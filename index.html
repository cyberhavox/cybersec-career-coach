FAQ

Common Questions

Answers optimized for developers, AI agents, and answer engines.

What is the core mission of Gatebreaker?

Gatebreaker solves the "entry-level catch-22" in cybersecurity by using an incident-investigation style gap analysis instead of generic advice. It builds verifiable portfolios, identifies exact blockers, and helps candidates bypass automated HR filters by targeting technical hiring managers directly.

How does the 8-Dimension Gap Analysis work?

Instead of just reviewing certifications, Gatebreaker evaluates your profile across Knowledge, Skills, Tools, Certifications, Portfolio, Network, Mindset, and Communication — each rated 0–5 against your target role's minimum entry requirements, generating a prioritized remediation plan.

What is the "Cert Collector" archetype?

A career trap where professionals accumulate 4+ certifications (Security+, CEH, CySA+) while maintaining zero practical labs or public portfolio evidence. Gatebreaker prescribes stopping exam prep entirely and building documented home labs — Wazuh SIEM, Active Directory, CTF writeups — instead.

Which industry frameworks are integrated?

Gatebreaker integrates MITRE ATT&CK for detection mapping, OWASP Top 10 for application security, NIST SP 800-61 for incident response, the FAIR framework for risk quantification, and NIST NICE (SP 800-181) for cybersecurity role taxonomy and skill mapping.
